About me

Hi! I’m Whitney Airewele, a final-year student specializing in Data Analytics at Dublin City University, with international experience from Kedge Business School in Marseille, France. My passion lies in leveraging data and technology to drive meaningful change, improve processes, and create efficiencies in diverse industries.

Throughout my career, I’ve gained hands-on experience in risk management, compliance, and fund services during internships at Impax Asset Management and Kestrel Capital. I’ve worked extensively with tools like Power BI, Excel, and SQL to analyze and visualize data, streamlining operations and ensuring regulatory compliance. My journey has also included dynamic team collaborations such as participating in a hackathon that sharpened my problem-solving and teamwork skills.

With certifications in Power BI, Python, Tableau, and statistical analysis, I stay at the forefront of technology and innovation. I’m also passionate about exploring trends in AI and finding ways to automate and improve workflows. Outside of work, I enjoy exploring natural wonders and embracing new cultures through my international experiences.
